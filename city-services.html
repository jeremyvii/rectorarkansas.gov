City Services

The City of Rector provides water utility services. Other amenities such as cable, telephone, internet, sanitation and gas services are provided by third party suppliers.

Rector Water Department

The Rector Water Department is located at 118 E. 4th St. For more information, please call (870) 595-3035.

City Maintenance

The city maintains a modern water/sewer system with three deep wells, a 250,000-gallon storage tank and a lagoon/sand-filter sanitation system. Additionally, the city recently purchased several generators to maintain the water/sewer system in the event of an emergency, as well as provide electricity for the city hall and community center.

Crist Engineers of Little Rock, AR serves as the city's engineer.

Animal Control

The city also maintains an animal control center. Animal Control Officer, David Jones is responsible for the facility and for responding for reports of stray animals. If your animal has been picked up by animal control, please contact city hall at (870) 595-3035. You will be required to pay a fee to retrieve your animal.

Crowleys' Ridge Veterinary Clinic is the city's veterinarian. For adoptions, please contact the clinic at (870) 598-5145. Both the city and the clinic are accepting donations to help with the care. Please contact city hall for more information.
