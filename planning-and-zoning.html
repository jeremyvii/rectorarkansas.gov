Planning and zoning in Rector is managed by the Planning Commission. The Planning Commission is a committee of five members who guides long-term development, and recommend zoning policy updates to City Council.

Planning and Zoning Documents

The Planning Commission meets as needed to discuss zoning matters and policy updates to Rector's zoning regulations. The Board of Zoning Adjustments hears requests for variances and appeals regarding administrative interpretations of zoning policy.
